The Studio

No camera. No studio. No limits.

Morsel Studio is a one-person creative practice exploring what commercial food photography becomes when the lens is replaced by a prompt. Every frame in this portfolio was lit, styled and rendered with generative AI — then graded like the real thing.

The brief is always the same: make it look good enough to put on a shelf. The packaging, the splash, the condensation, the catchlights — all synthetic, all intentional.
